AAj is a SensorialBrainualConcept--Computer-System (SBCCS) which uses my AAj-program, a free and open-source sensorial-b-concept--program written in java computer-language and representing sensorial-b-concepts using XML.
AAj help us to MANAGE (= store, retrieve, validate) BRAINUAL-WORLDVIEWS, of different AUTHORS, in different HUMAN-LANGUAGES. #h0p1#

It is the outcome of about 25 years of hard work, or of about 50,000 work-hours (when a life-long (35 years) full-time (8 hours) job is equal to about 60,000 work-hours). #h0p2#

Also, I present here my sensorial-brainual-concept--theory which includes a theory-of-language and my Komo constructed human-language. AAj, from another view, also is an implementation of my sensorial-b-concept--theory. #h0p3#

This is NOT a finished (*) work. Rather, it is the START of a new method of storing and manipulating consistent/coherent/integrated-information/knowledge. #h0p4#

I expect only one thing: to help in the improvement of our society.
I hope, AAj to become the tool we need to create the "NEW-VISION = open-networked-planned-economy" of a better society. [2008.11.16] #h0p5#
